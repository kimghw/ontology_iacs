
# D7 Watertight integrity

(1979)
(Rev.1 1990)
(Rev.2 1996)
(Rev.3 Jan 2012)

## D7.1 Watertight boundaries

D7.1.1 All units are to be provided with watertight bulkheads as may be required by the Rules.

## D7.2 Tank boundaries

D7.2.1 Tanks for fresh water or fuel oil are to have divisions or deep swashes as may be required.

## D7.3 Boundary penetrations

D7.3.1 Where watertight boundaries are required for damage stability, they are to be made watertight throughout.

## D7.4 Closures

### D7.4.1 General

External closing appliances are to be as prescribed by applicable load line requirements.

### D7.4.2 General requirements related to watertight integrity

External openings which are used during operation of the unit while afloat, are not to submerge when the unit is inclined to the first intercept of the righting moment and wind heeling moment curves.

### D7.4.3 General requirements related to weathertight integrity

Any opening having its lower edge submerged below a waterline associated with the stability zones is to be fitted with a weathertight closing appliance.
