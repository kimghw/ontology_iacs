
# D5 Column stabilized drilling units

(1979)
(Rev. 1 1987)
(Rev. 2 1990)
(Rev. 3 1996)

### D5.1 General

D5.1.1 This section applies to the unit type as defined in D2.2.2

### D5.2 Upper structure

D5.2.1 The scantlings of the upper structure are not to be less than those required by the Rules.

### D5.3 Columns, lower hulls and footings

D5.3.1 Main stability columns, lower hulls or footings may be designed as either framed or unframed shells.

### D5.4 Wave clearance

D5.4.1 Unless deck structures are designed for wave impact, reasonable clearance between the deck structures and the wave crests is to be ensured.

### D5.5 Structural Redundancy

D5.5.1 The unit's structure shall be able to withstand the loss of any slender bracing member without causing overall collapse.

### D5.6 Damage Stability

D5.6.1 Damage assumptions for column stabilized drilling units as required by D3.7.3.
