## Regulation 1

### Application

Regulation 1 reads as follows:

*The provisions of this Annex shall apply to all ships, except where expressly provided otherwise.*

### Interpretation

For application of this regulation the term "all ships" shall be interpreted as applicable to all ships as defined by MARPOL 73 Article 2 (4).
