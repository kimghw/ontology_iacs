# MPC 30 (July 2004) (Rev.1 Nov 2019)

## Technical Code on Control of Emission of Nitrogen Oxides from Marine Diesel Engines
(NOx Technical Code 2008, Table 3 – Symbols and subscripts for terms and variables)

Table 3 - Symbols and subscripts for terms and variables (refer to chapter 5, chapter 6, appendix 4 and appendix 6 of this Code)

Table 3 gives:

| Symbol | Term | Unit |
|---|---|---|
| Ta | Intake air temperature determined at the engine intake | K |

### Interpretation

For application of the term "Ta" it shall be interpreted that the intake air temperature determined at the engine intake is that determined at the engine / turbocharger intake suction filter.
