# Watertight door closure
**SC 147**
(May 1999)
(Rev.1 Nov 2005)
(Rev.2 Feb 2021)

**FSS Code, Ch. 9, 2.1.2.4**

the system may be arranged with output signals to other fire safety systems including:

[...]
.3 Fire doors
[...]

### Interpretation
Watertight doors complying with Reg.II-1/16 which also serve as fire doors are not to close automatically in case of fire detection.

---

### Note:
1.  This UI SC 147 is to be uniformly implemented by IACS Members and Associates from 1 January 2000.
2.  The "contract for construction" date means the date on which the contract to build the vessel is signed between the prospective owner and the shipbuilder. For further details regarding the date of "contract for construction", refer to IACS Procedural Requirement (PR) No.29.
