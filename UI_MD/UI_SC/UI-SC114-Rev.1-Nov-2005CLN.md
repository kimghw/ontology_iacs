**SC114 Emergency Fire Pump Access**
(1996)
(Rev.1 Nov 2005)

(Reg. II-2/10.2.2.3.2.1)

When a single access to the emergency fire pump room is through another space adjoining a machinery space of category A or the spaces containing the main fire pumps, class A-60 boundary is required between that other space and the machinery space of category A or the spaces containing the main fire pumps.
