# SC41 Means of Escape
(Rev.1
2001)
(Rev.2
Nov 2005)

(Reg. II-2/13.4.1.3)

## Text:
Reg. II-2/13.4.1.3: " ...In a ship of 1,000 gross tonnage and above, the Administration may dispense with one means of escape from any such space, including a normally unattended auxiliary machinery space, so long as either a door or a steel ladder provides a safe escape route to the embarkation deck, due regard being paid to the nature and location of the space and whether persons are normally employed in that space."

## Interpretation:
The above requirement applies only to auxiliary machinery spaces where persons are not normally employed.
