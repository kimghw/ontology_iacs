## SC64 Fire dampers in ventilation ducts
(Rev.1
Nov 2005)

(Reg. II-2/9.7.3.1)

Ducts or pipes with free sectional area of 0,075m² or less need not be fitted with fire damper at their passage through Class "A" divisions provided that the requirements of 9.7.2.1, 9.7.2.2, 9.4.1.1.8, 9.3.3 and 9.7.4.3 are complied with.
