# SC92 Personal Protection - Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus
(Rev.1 Nov 2005)

(Reg. II-2/19.3.6.2)

For each of the breathing apparatuses, two complete sets of air bottles are required. These spare bottles are to be in addition to the spare bottles required for fireman's outfit.
