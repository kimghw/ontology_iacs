## Closing appliances of ventilation inlets and outlets

(Reg. II-2/5.2.1.1)

Ventilation inlets and outlets located at outside boundaries are to be fitted with closing appliances as required by Reg. II-2/5.2.1.1 and need not comply with Reg. II-2/9.7.3.1.
