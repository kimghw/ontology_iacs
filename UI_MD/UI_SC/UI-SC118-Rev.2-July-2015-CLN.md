# SC 118
## Exhaust duct from galley ranges
(1996)
(Rev.1 Nov 2005)
(Rev.2 July 2015)

(Reg. II-2/9.7.5.1.1 and 9.7.5.2.1)

Fire dampers required by Reg. II-2/9.7.5.1.1 and 9.7.5.2.1 do not need to pass the fire test in either Res. A 754(18) or Appendix 2 of Part 3, of Annex 1 of the 2010 FTP Code, but should be of steel and capable of stopping the draught. The requirements to "A" class applies only to the part of the duct outside of the galley.

Note:
1. This UI is to be uniformly implemented by IACS Members on ships constructed before 1 January 2016.
