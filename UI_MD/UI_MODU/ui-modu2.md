# MODU 2 (Aug 2016)

## Inclusion of mediums of the fire-fighting systems in lightweight (2009 MODU Code Chapter 1, paragraph 1.3.30)

### Regulation

**2009 MODU Code Chapter 1, paragraph 1.3.30**

"Lightweight is the displacement of a unit in tonnes without variable deck load, fuel, lubricating oil, ballast water, fresh water and feedwater in tanks, consumable stores, and personnel and their effects."

### Interpretation

The weight of mediums on board for the fixed fire-fighting systems (e.g. freshwater, CO2, dry chemical powder, foam concentrate, etc.) shall be included in the lightweight.

### Note:

1.  This Unified Interpretation is to be uniformly implemented by IACS Societies on ships contracted for construction on or after 1 January 2017.
2.  The "contracted for construction" date means the date on which the contract to build the vessel is signed between the prospective owner and the shipbuilder. For further details regarding the date of "contract for construction", refer to IACS Procedural Requirement (PR) No. 29.
