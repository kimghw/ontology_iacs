LL25
Effective length of raised quarterdeck
(1972) (Rev.1 July 2008)
(Regulation 35(4))

It is recommended that the maximum effective length of 0.6L of a raised quarterdeck which is stipulated by Regulation 35(4), is to be measured from the after perpendicular even where a poop is fitted in conjunction with the raised quarterdeck.

Footnote: This UI is also applicable to Regulation 35(4) of the 1988 Protocol.
