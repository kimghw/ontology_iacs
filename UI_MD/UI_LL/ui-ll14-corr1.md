# LL14 Protection of the crew (Regulation 25(2))

(1968)
(Rev.1 July 2008)
(Corr.1 Oct 2015)

## Regulation 25(2)

(2) Efficient guard rails or bulwarks shall be fitted to all exposed parts of the freeboard and superstructure decks. The height of the bulwarks or guard rails shall be at least 1 metre (39 ½ inches) from the deck, provided that where this height would interfere with the normal operation of the ship, a lesser height may be approved if the Administration is satisfied that adequate protection is provided.

## Interpretation

A guard rail should also be required for first tier deckhouses and for superstructures' ends.

Footnote: This UI is also applicable to Regulation 25(2) of the 1988 Protocol.
