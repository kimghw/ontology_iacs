# LL31 Deduction for excess sheer (Regulation 38(15))

(1972)
(Rev.1
July 2008)

Since no stipulation is made as to the height of the superstructure referred to in Regulation 38(15), it is recommended that the height of this superstructure shall be related to its standard height. When the height of the superstructure or raised quarterdeck is less than standard, the reduction shall be in the ratio of the actual to the standard height thereof.

---
**Footnote:** This UI is applicable to Regulation 38(15) of the 1988 Protocol.
