## Interpretation

For the purposes of the application of the IMO Conventions and Codes (Performance Standards, Technical Standards, Resolutions and Circulars) for Fibre-Reinforced Plastic (FRP) Craft, the term "the keels of which are laid or which are at a similar stage of construction" should be interpreted as the date that the first structural reinforcement of the complete thickness of the approved hull laminate schedule is laid either in or on the mould.

## NOTE
1. This UI is to be uniformly implemented by IACS Societies from 1 January 2014.
