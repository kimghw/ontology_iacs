# LL22 Position of the inboard end of discharges when timber freeboard is assigned (Regulation 22(1))

(1972)
(Rev.1 July 2008)

It is considered that the position of the inboard end of discharges should be related to the timber summer load waterline when timber freeboard is assigned.

---

### Footnotes:
1. This Ul is also applicable to Regulation 22(1) of the 1988 Protocol.
2. This Ul is also applicable to Regulation 22-1 of the revised 1988 Protocol.
