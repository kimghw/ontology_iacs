# Z8 Corrosion Protection Coating for Salt Water Ballast Spaces

**Z8**
(1990)
(Rev 1 1995)

At the time of new construction, all salt water ballast spaces having boundaries formed by the hull envelope shall have an efficient protective coating, epoxy or equivalent, applied in accordance with the manufacturer's recommendations.
