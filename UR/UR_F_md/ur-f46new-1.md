# F46 Low pressure CO<sub>2</sub> piping system

(Aug 2021)

**(FSS Code Ch.5.2.2)**

Where a low-pressure CO<sub>2</sub> system is fitted, the piping system is to be designed in such a way that the CO<sub>2</sub> pressure at the nozzles should not be less than 1 N/mm<sup>2</sup>.

Note:

1. This Unified Requirement is to be uniformly implemented by IACS Societies on ships constructed on or after 1 July 2022.
