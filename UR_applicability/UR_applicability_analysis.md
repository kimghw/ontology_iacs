Total Extracted URs: 102 (valid URs, excluding Deleted/Withdrawn)

## A Series (Hull Fittings)

### UR A2 - Shipboard fittings and supporting hull structures associated with towing and mooring on conventional ships
**Source:** IACS UR A2 (Rev.5 Sep 2020)
**Scope:** Design and construction of shipboard fittings and supporting structures used for normal towing and mooring operations.
**Applicable to:** Conventional ships = new displacement-type ships of **500 GT and above**, excluding high speed craft, special purpose ships, and offshore units.
**Not applicable to:** Escort towing, Canal transit towing, Emergency towing for tankers
**Implementation:** Rev.5 → contracted for construction on or after 1 January 2022

### UR A3 - Anchor Windlass Design and Testing
**Source:** IACS UR A3
**Scope:** Windlass used for handling anchors, suitable for the size of chain cable.
**Implementation:** Application for certification on or after 1 July 2018; Rev.1 → on or after 1 July 2020

## F Series (Fire Safety)

### UR F5 - Pump room alarms
**Source:** IACS UR F5 (Rev.1 1973)
**Scope:** Audible alarms fitted to warn of fire extinguishing medium release into pump rooms

### UR F6 - Standardization of flash points
**Source:** IACS UR F6 (Rev.1 1996)
**Scope:** Oil tankers = vessels capable of carrying oil having a flash point not exceeding 60 deg C (closed cup test)

### UR F7 - Portable instruments for measuring oxygen and flammable vapour concentrations
**Source:** IACS UR F7 (Rev.3 June 2020)
**Scope:** Every oil tanker - minimum 2 portable gas detectors (%LEL) + 2 portable O2 analysers; inert gas system tankers require additional detectors
**Implementation:** Rev.3 → ships contracted on or after 1 July 2021

### UR F8 - Pressurisation of cargo tanks
**Source:** IACS UR F8 (Rev.1 1989)
**Scope:** PV valves to oil tanks - pressure not exceeding 0.21 bar unless tank scantlings specially considered

### UR F20.4 - Nitrogen/Inert Gas Systems for tankers
**Source:** IACS UR F20.4
**Scope:** Systems fitted on oil tankers, gas tankers or chemical tankers to which SOLAS II-2/4.5.5.1 and 4.5.5.2 do not apply

### UR F44 - Fore peak ballast tanks on oil & chemical tankers
**Source:** IACS UR F44
**Implementation:** Rev.2 → ships contracted on or after 1 January 2012; Rev.3 → on or after 1 January 2026

### UR F45 - Installation of BWMS on-board ships
**Source:** IACS UR F45
**Scope:** Fire safety measures for BWMS installation onboard any ship (UV, Flocculation, De-oxygenation, Electrolysis, Chemical injection, Ozone injection, Pasteurization, etc.)
**Implementation:** Ships contracted on or after 1 July 2022

## Z Series (Survey)

### UR Z1 - Annual and Intermediate Classification Survey Coverage
**Source:** IACS UR Z1
**Scope:** IMO Res. A.1186(33) HSSC 2023 requirements covered by classification surveys

### UR Z3 - Periodical Survey of Ship's Bottom
**Source:** IACS UR Z3
**Scope:** All ships - bottom examinations normally in drydock; in-water survey considered (special consideration for ships >= 15 years)

### UR Z6 - Continuous System for Hull Special Survey
**Source:** IACS UR Z6
**Scope:** Ships other than those subject to UR Z10s and UR Z7.1 - at owner's request and Society's agreement

### UR Z7 - Hull Classification Surveys
**Source:** IACS UR Z7
**Scope:** All self-propelled vessels

### UR Z7.1 - Hull Surveys for General Dry Cargo Ships
**Source:** IACS UR Z7.1
**Scope:** All self-propelled General Dry Cargo Ships of **500 GT and above** carrying solid cargoes (excluding container carriers, ro-ro, reefer, wood chip, cement, livestock, deck cargo, and double-side skin ships)

### UR Z7.2 - Hull Surveys for Liquefied Gas Carriers
**Source:** IACS UR Z7.2
**Scope:** All self-propelled ships carrying **liquefied gases in bulk** - hull structure and piping (except Z16 covered piping)
**Implementation:** On or after 1 July 2008

### UR Z8 - Corrosion Protection Coating for Salt Water Ballast Spaces
**Source:** IACS UR Z8 (Rev.1 1995)
**Scope:** At time of new construction, all salt water ballast spaces having boundaries formed by the hull envelope - epoxy or equivalent coating

### UR Z9 - Corrosion Protection Coatings for Cargo Hold Spaces on Bulk Carriers
**Source:** IACS UR Z9 (Rev.2 1996)
**Scope:** All internal/external surfaces of hatch coamings/covers and all internal surfaces of cargo holds on **bulk carriers** (excluding flat tank top and hopper sloping plating ~300 mm below side shell frames)
**Implementation:** Rev.2 → request for class on or after 1 July 1998

### UR Z10.1 - Hull Surveys of Oil Tankers
**Source:** IACS UR Z10.1
**Scope:** All self-propelled Oil Tankers **other than** Double Hull Oil Tankers (→ Z10.4)
**Not applicable to:** Asphalt carriers with independent tanks

### UR Z10.2 - Hull Surveys of Bulk Carriers
**Source:** IACS UR Z10.2
**Scope:** All self-propelled Bulk Carriers **other than** Double Skin Bulk Carriers (→ Z10.5). Hybrid arrangements: Z10.2 for single skin holds, Z10.5 for double skin holds.

### UR Z10.3 - Hull Surveys of Chemical Tankers
**Source:** IACS UR Z10.3
**Scope:** All self-propelled Chemical Tankers with **integral tanks**
**Not applicable to:** Combined gas/chemical carriers with independent tanks (→ survey as gas carriers); independent tanks on deck (→ Z7)

### UR Z10.4 - Hull Surveys of Double Hull Oil Tankers
**Source:** IACS UR Z10.4
**Scope:** All self-propelled **Double Hull Oil Tankers** - hull structure and piping. CSR/non-CSR ships have different thickness measurement and acceptance criteria.

### UR Z10.5 - Hull Surveys of Double Skin Bulk Carriers
**Source:** IACS UR Z10.5
**Scope:** All self-propelled **Double Skin Bulk Carriers**. Hybrid arrangements: single skin holds → Z10.2.
**Additional:** Ships > 20 years, >= 150 m → additional annual examination of double-side skin void spaces

### UR Z11 - Mandatory Ship Type and ESP Notations
**Source:** IACS UR Z11
**Scope:** Oil tankers, chemical tankers, bulk carriers subject to enhanced survey programme (Z10.1~Z10.5). Defines mandatory "ESP" class notations for Oil Tanker, Bulk Carrier, Ore Carrier, Ore/Oil Carrier, OBO Carrier, Self-Unloading BC, Chemical Tanker.

### UR Z13 - Voyage Repairs and Maintenance
**Source:** IACS UR Z13 (Rev.3 Jan 2011)
**Scope:** Where repairs to hull, machinery or equipment affecting classification are carried out by riding crew during a voyage - must be planned in advance and notified to Classification Society. Failure to notify may result in suspension of class.
**Implementation:** Rev.3 → surveys commenced on or after 1 July 2011

### UR Z15 - Surveys of Mobile Offshore Drilling Units
**Source:** IACS UR Z15
**Scope:** All **Mobile Offshore Drilling Units** after construction - hull, structure, equipment and machinery subject to classification. For Surface-Type Units, replaces Z1, Z3, Z6, Z7, Z18, Z21.
**Implementation:** Rev.3 → surveys commenced on or after 1 July 2020

### UR Z16 - Periodical Surveys of Cargo Installations on Liquefied Gas Carriers
**Source:** IACS UR Z16
**Scope:** Ships designed for carriage of **liquefied gases in bulk** - cargo installations (additional to Z1 and Z7)
**Not applicable to:** Fire protection, fire-fighting, portable equipment, personnel protection

### UR Z17 - Procedural Requirements for Service Suppliers
**Source:** IACS UR Z17
**Scope:** Approval of service suppliers for statutory and classification services (liferaft servicing, radio equipment, breathing apparatus, VDR testing, thickness measurements, in-water surveys, fire extinguishing, etc.)
**Not applicable to (thickness measurement exemption):** Non-ESP ships < 500 GT and all fishing vessels

### UR Z18 - Survey of Machinery
**Source:** IACS UR Z18
**Scope:** Machinery surveys - special consideration for government vessels in military operations

### UR Z19 - Calibration of Measuring Equipment
**Source:** IACS UR Z19 (April 1999)
**Scope:** Inspection, measuring and test equipment which Surveyors rely on for classification/statutory certification decisions - to be individually identified and calibrated to recognized standards

### UR Z20 - Planned Maintenance Scheme (PMS) for Machinery
**Source:** IACS UR Z20
**Scope:** Approved PMS as alternative to Continuous Machinery Survey (CMS) - limited to components/systems covered by CMS
**Implementation:** Rev.2 → surveys commenced on or after 1 July 2020

### UR Z21 - Surveys of Propeller Shafts and Tube Shafts
**Source:** IACS UR Z21
**Scope:** All vessels with conventional shafting fitted with a propeller (unless alternative means provided)
**Implementation:** From 1 January 2016 for ships delivered on or after 1 January 2016

### UR Z23 - Hull Survey for New Construction
**Source:** IACS UR Z23
**Scope:** All new construction of **steel ships** for classification and international voyages
**Not applicable to:** SOLAS I/3 ships; HSC per 2000 HSC Code; MODUs per MODU Code; manufacture of equipment/fittings/appendages (hatch covers, doors, rudders, forgings/castings)
**Implementation:** Rev.7 → contracted on or after 1 July 2021

### UR Z24 - Survey of Ro-Ro Ship Doors
**Source:** IACS UR Z24
**Scope:** Bow, inner, side shell and stern doors of **Ro-Ro passenger (Ro-Pax) and Ro-Ro cargo ships**
**Implementation:** Surveys commenced on or after 1 January 2012

### UR Z25 - Periodic Survey of Fuel Installations (Gas/Low Flashpoint Fuels)
**Source:** IACS UR Z25
**Scope:** Ships utilizing gas or other low flash point fuels for propulsion/auxiliary power (other than Z16 covered vessels)
**Not applicable to:** Fire protection, fire-fighting, personnel protection equipment
**Implementation:** Rev.1 → surveys commenced on or after 1 January 2019

### UR Z26 - Alternative Certification Scheme (ACS)
**Source:** IACS UR Z26 (Feb 2015)
**Scope:** ACS may be arranged with product manufacturers and/or sub-suppliers - permits manufacturer to carry out inspection/testing without Surveyor presence
**Implementation:** Uniformly implemented on or after 1 July 2016

### UR Z27 - Condition Monitoring and Condition Based Maintenance
**Source:** IACS UR Z27
**Scope:** Approved CM/CBM schemes where monitoring results influence scope/frequency of Class survey - applicable only to vessels operating on approved PMS scheme
**Implementation:** On or after 1 January 2020

### UR Z28 - Surveys of Watertight Cable Transits
**Source:** IACS UR Z28
**Scope:** All vessels and MOUs - additional to Z23, Z7, Z15
**Implementation:** Contracted on or after 1 July 2021

### UR Z29 - Remote Classification Surveys
**Source:** IACS UR Z29
**Scope:** All vessels, self-propelled or not
**Not applicable to (mandatory):** Offshore units
**Implementation:** Surveys commenced on or after 1 January 2023
