This bundle narrows the search to three slices:

- `biomedical`
- `materials`
- `semantic-web`

Selection notes:

- I prioritized open-access PDFs with a direct relation to ontology learning, ontology engineering, ontology mapping, ontology-grounded knowledge graph construction, or ontology-adjacent normalization/relation tasks.
- Some materials papers are closer to `knowledge graph / ontology grounding` than to classic OWL ontology induction, but they fit your use case because they operationalize structured semantics with LLM pipelines in the materials domain.
- Some semantic-web papers are review or capability-assessment papers rather than end-to-end construction papers; I kept them because they are currently among the clearest recent references for the ontology engineering side of the space.

The full paper list, grouped with domain and category labels, is in [`urls.tsv`](./urls.tsv).
