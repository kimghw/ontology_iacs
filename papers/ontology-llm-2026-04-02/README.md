# Ontology Learning / Ontology Engineering LLM Paper Bundle

Date: 2026-04-02

Location: `/home/kimghw/iacs/papers/ontology-llm-2026-04-02`

This bundle collects recent open-access PDFs around LLM-driven ontology learning and ontology engineering, centered on:

- prompt-based ontology construction pipelines
- term extraction and term typing
- taxonomy discovery
- non-taxonomic relation extraction
- ontology or lexical-resource relation prediction
- terminology-aware translation as the closest recent open-access proxy for SKOS thesaurus translation

Selection notes:

- Most ontology-learning papers come from the `LLMs4OL 2024` and `LLMs4OL 2025` challenge series because they map directly to the task breakdown you asked for.
- I also added a few broader papers on end-to-end ontology learning, life-science ontology learning, lexical-resource relation prediction, and terminology translation.
- I did not find a clearly recent, open-access paper that is specifically about `LLM + SKOS thesaurus translation`. The translation papers in this bundle are adjacent recent work on terminology-constrained or terminology-aware translation.

Files are listed in [`urls.tsv`](./urls.tsv) with year, category, title, and source URL.

Bundle contents:

- `01` to `07`: LLMs4OL 2024/2025 overview and benchmark papers
- `08` to `11`: task-focused LLMs4OL participant papers on prompting, RAG, and term typing
- `12` to `13`: broader ontology-learning papers (`End-to-End Ontology Learning with LLMs`, `LLMs4Life`)
- `14`: lexical-resource relation prediction (`Multiple Hypernymy Resolution in WordNet`)
- `15` to `18`: recent terminology-aware translation papers
